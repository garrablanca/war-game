GAME OF WAR

Simple Rules:
1.-Every Player starts whit 26 cards out of 52 cards deck, very well mixed.
2.-In every round played, the Player with the greatest value card, wins the round.
3.-Only If in the round you got tie, the cards stays on table until in the next round one of the players will win; then the round winner collects all the points and cards.
4.-The ultimate winner for every deal is that one who will left the opponent whitout any card, regardless the total points.
4a.- Except if you limit the game to a 100 rounds then the player with more points will win the game.
